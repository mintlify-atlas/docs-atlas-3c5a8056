## What is Helium?

Helium is a privacy-focused Chromium-based web browser built with a simple philosophy: **respect for users first**. Based on the excellent [ungoogled-chromium](https://github.com/ungoogled-software/ungoogled-chromium) project, Helium strips away Google's bloat and surveillance while delivering a fast, clean browsing experience.

## Why Choose Helium?

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Privacy-First Architecture">
    Helium removes all Google services and tracking mechanisms from Chromium. No analytics, no crash reporting, no background connections to Google servers. Your privacy is protected by default, not as an opt-in feature.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Built on ungoogled-chromium">
    Standing on the shoulders of giants, Helium leverages the ungoogled-chromium project to provide a solid, de-Googled foundation. This means you get all the performance and web compatibility of Chromium without the privacy concerns.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Open Source & Transparent">
    All Helium code is open source under GPL-3.0. You can inspect, modify, and contribute to the browser. Platform-specific packaging is also fully open source:
    - [Helium for macOS](https://github.com/imputnet/helium-macos)
    - [Helium for Linux](https://github.com/imputnet/helium-linux)
    - [Helium for Windows](https://github.com/imputnet/helium-windows)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="No Bloatware">
    Helium doesn't come with unnecessary features, bundled services, or promotional content. It's a browser, not a platform. Clean, simple, and respectful of your system resources.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
